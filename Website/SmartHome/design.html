Design

The Smart Home Project is designed to provide automation to outlets, temperature, sprinklers and garage. As well as monitoring outlet power consumption, temperature, and room occupancy. These controls are to be accessable from a secure website, a secure mobile website, texting, or from voice recognition within the home. The implemenation of these features will provide greater home energy efficency and greater comfort.
